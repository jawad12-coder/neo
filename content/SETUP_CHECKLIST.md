# Private launch checklist

- Final logo and confirmed brand colours
- Company email and business hours
- Founder name, history, year established and verified milestones
- Professional registrations and confirmed service options
- Social links and team profiles
- Genuine project photography, project facts and permissions
- Verified client testimonials
- Terms, warranty, payment and consultation policies
- Confirmed service areas and map / Google Business links
- Email provider, secure lead storage, file scanning and upload storage
- Analytics, consent mode and advertising configuration
- Legal review of privacy policy and terms

Do not publish bracketed placeholders, sample projects, draft posts or editorial imagery as verified company content.
